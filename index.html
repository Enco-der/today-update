Experience

With over 25+ years of experience in residential and commercial real estate, Mike Koen has helped

Mike is more than a real estate agent. He is also a Licensed Builder and has served as a Home Inspector
for the State of NJ. After years in the field, he uses his experience to help owners and investors navigate
the complexities of NJ real estate and lending.
